Facts about Theni

Theni is one of the district head quarters of Tamilnadu state in India . It is situated at the foothills of western ghats. It is a commercial town and its population is around 12 lakhs. It is connected with ohter cities by road and train. Nearest airport is in Madurai.

Did you know?

Theni is also famous for its big weekly vegetable market held on every Saturday. Villagers from sourrending villages come and sell their vegetables.

Some tourist attractions

  • Penny Cuick Memorial
  • Vaigai dam and Kumbakarai falls in Periyakulam Taluk
  • Suruli falls in Uthamapalayam Taluk
  • Gowmariamman Temple at Veerapandi Village (TheniTaluk)
  • Kannagi temple which witnesses as single day festivity during Chitra Pournami day is in the inter state border.
